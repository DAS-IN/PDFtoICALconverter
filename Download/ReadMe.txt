# How to convert?
1. open KU campus and click on "Mein Stundenplan"
2. select the timetable and timeframe you want to export, afterwards click "Suchen"
3. in the area of "Meine Veranstaltungstermine" there is a printer symbol on the right upper edge, click on it and execute "Ausgabe als PDF"
4. save the PDF
5. open PDFtoICALConverter
6. determine where the PDF is saved and where you want to save the ical
7. check what you want to import (all = name of lecture & time & lecturer name & room | name of lecture & time & name of lecturer | name of lecture & time & room)
8. click ok
9. thank the contributors in the next window
10. click ok - look at the location of the ical file if it was generated
11. import where you want it
